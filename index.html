About Mina

Stories don't begin with the crime.
They begin with the wound.

Mina Dawaar writes psychological thrillers that explore trauma, obsession, memory, abandonment, love, and the quiet ways people learn to survive.

Her novels follow ordinary people whose lives begin to fracture beneath the weight of long-buried secrets, unresolved grief, impossible choices, and the stories they tell themselves to keep going.

Every novel asks not only what happened — but why.

Mina Dawaar

She lives in Canada.
