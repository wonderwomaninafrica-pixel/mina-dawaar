The Pale Room

N°01 — Out Now

The heat is real even when the lover might not be.

She has a stable marriage, a quiet apartment, and a recurring dream of a room she has never seen — until a stranger she's met only online starts describing it back to her, detail for detail.

The Pale Room follows what happens once that connection stops feeling like a coincidence.
